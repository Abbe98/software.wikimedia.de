Wikidata

The free knowledge base that anyone can edit

Wikidata is a free and open knowledge base that can be read and edited by both humans and machines. Wikidata provides central storage for the structured data of its Wikimedia sister projects including Wikipedia, Wikivoyage, Wikisource, and others. Wikidata also provides support to many other sites and services beyond just Wikimedia projects. The content of Wikidata is freely available, can be exported using standard formats, and can be interlinked to other open data sets on the linked data web.
